About the Data

This dataset was taken from Kaggle.com and it is a compilation of every shot that Kobe Bryant took during his 20 year career in the NBA. As you can see, the dataset is quite extensive. I have attempted to analyze and visualize this data in order to better understand Kobe's shot selection.
